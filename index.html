About us

Solability is a solar energy company based in Miami, FL. With a commitment to sustainability and a passion for renewable energy, we strive to provide innovative solar solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our team of experts combines extensive industry knowledge with cutting-edge technology to deliver cost-effective and environmentally friendly solar power systems. Whether you are looking to reduce your carbon footprint, lower your energy bills, or increase the value of your property, Solability is here to help you harness the power of the sun and make a positive impact on the planet.

Gowri Kanugovi is the owner and founder of Solability. She is passionate about solar energy's potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and create jobs. She believes that solar is the future of energy and is committed to helping people go solar and save money on their energy bills.
